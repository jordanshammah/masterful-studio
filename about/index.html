About Masterful

Masterful is Jordan Shammah's visibility studio. Built in Nairobi in 2023 because the AI search transition was happening faster than any agency was moving — and most of the market was still selling last decade's playbook.


The problem isn't that businesses are bad at SEO. It's that the search surface changed underneath them. Google is still important. But ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini now answer the questions that used to drive your traffic. If you're not in those answers, you don't exist for an entire generation of buyers. That's the gap we fix.
