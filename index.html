The Situation

The search tools your customers use have changed.

Ten years ago, showing up on Google was enough. You built your website, collected reviews, and ranked. That model still works. But something has shifted.

A growing number of your potential customers now start their search by asking an AI tool a question. They type "best boutique hotel in Karen" or "best safari company in Nairobi" and they get a direct answer, not a list of websites to scroll through. Two or three businesses are named. They book the first one that sounds right.

The businesses being recommended are not always the best ones. They are the ones structured so AI tools can read, understand, and recommend them.

If your website is not set up that way, AI tools skip you. Your competitors get the recommendation. You lose the booking before the customer ever finds your website.

We set up your online presence so search tools, both Google and AI tools, recommend you by name.

Frequently Asked

What exactly is AEO? +
AEO stands for Answer Engine Optimisation. When someone asks an AI tool a question, the tool gives a direct answer with specific business names. AEO is the work that gets your business into those answers. Think of it as SEO, but for AI tools instead of Google.
Do I need to understand the technical side? +
No. We explain everything in plain language. You tell us the result you want. We tell you what we are going to do and why. You approve it and we do the work.
How long before I see results? +
The first changes to your AI recommendation presence typically show within 30 to 60 days. Google rankings take longer, usually 60 to 90 days depending on how competitive your market is.
I already pay for Google Ads. Why do I need this? +
Google Ads buy visibility. This work earns it. When your ad budget stops, paid visibility stops immediately. The search rankings and AI recommendations we build continue bringing customers long after the work is done.
Do you work with businesses outside Kenya? +
Yes. A portion of our clients are based in the UK and US. We work fully remotely and async. No calls required if that works better for you.
What if I am not happy with the results? +
Three-month minimum on retainers. If results are not showing after month one, we have a direct conversation before we continue. No hiding behind long contracts.
