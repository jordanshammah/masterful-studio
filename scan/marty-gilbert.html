IIWhat this means

You own your name. You lose the category.

AI tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity answer the questions senior professionals ask before they hire a job search coach. The wording of the question decides who makes the list. NSENG wins one question and loses the rest.

We ran six questions across both platforms. NSENG ranked for a single search: 'What the Hell Approach to Job Search', its branded methodology. It missed every category search an executive runs. ChatGPT returned InterviewNerds and CareerHigher. Perplexity returned Bernadette Pawlik and Endeavor Executive.

The branded search reaches executives who know NSENG. The category search reaches the ones who do not. New members start with the category search, and those answers point to other coaches.
