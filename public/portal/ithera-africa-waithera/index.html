§ V — The Root Cause

Why Ithera is invisible, and why it is fixable.

The problem is not the quality of Ithera's work. The problem is that none of it is formatted in a way that search engines and AI platforms can read. Think of it this way: Ithera has a superb library, but the catalogue is empty. The books are there. The index cards are missing.

No meta descriptions on any page.

Meta descriptions tell Google what each page is about. Without them, Google writes its own summary, usually poorly.

No structured data (JSON-LD schema) on any page.

Structured data is how you tell AI engines "this is a legal service, this is the founder, these are the services offered." Without it, AI ignores you.

No Open Graph images.

When someone shares an Ithera link on WhatsApp or LinkedIn, no preview image appears. Missing previews reduce click-through by 40 to 60%.

Blog posts authored as "Ithera" instead of "Waithera Mugo".

Google and AI look for named human authors with verifiable credentials. "Ithera" is generic. "Waithera Mugo, Advocate of the High Court" is authority.

No Google Business Profile.

When someone searches "tax advisory near me" in Nairobi, the local map results cannot show Ithera.

Half the published content is hidden.

The Resources page shows 6 of 13 published articles. Seven of Ithera's strongest pieces are published but invisible.

Homepage loads 30 stylesheets and 27 scripts.

Industry benchmark: 4 to 8 stylesheets. Current load time: 4.5 to 7 seconds on Nairobi 4G.

WordPress "Sample Page" still live and indexed.

The default WordPress placeholder is in the sitemap. To a serious buyer, it reads as unfinished.
